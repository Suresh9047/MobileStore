DESCRIPTION

The moto g04s boasts a 50 MP main camera for sharp, instantly focused photos with HDR, Night Vision, and Portrait mode, plus a 5 MP front camera for social media-ready selfies. Its sleek, lightweight design features a premium matte finish, weighing just 178 g and measuring 7.99 mm thin. The 16.66 cm (6.6), 90 Hz punch-hole display, protected by Gorilla Glass 3, offers immersive viewing with High Brightness and Night Light modes. Powered by the UNISOC T606 chipset, 4 GB RAM, and 64 GB storage, it ensures seamless performance and multitasking, with up to 8GB RAM Boost. The 5000 mAh battery provides extensive usage with rapid charging.
