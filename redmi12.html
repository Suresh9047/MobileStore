PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Redmi 12 5G mobile comes with a 90 Hz refresh rate 6.79-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 2460x1080 pixels (FHD+). The display sports Gorilla Glass for protection. Redmi 12 5G is powered by an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 4 Gen 2 processor. It comes with 8GB RAM. The Redmi 12 5G runs Android 13 and is powered by a 5000mAh non-removable battery.
